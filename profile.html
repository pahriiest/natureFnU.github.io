Nature FnU

Nature FnU is a trading company specializing in high-quality spices and coffee, working with farmers across Indonesia.

About Us

We are a trading company located in Tangerang, Banten, selling spices and coffee, including Ginger, Turmeric, Galangal, Black Pepper, White Pepper, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Cardamom, Clove, Arabica Greenbean, Robusta Greenbean, Arabica Roastbean, and Robusta Roastbean. We offer products in various qualities to meet customer needs.

We collaborate with farmers across Indonesia to ensure the supply and quality of products desired by customers. Committed to delivering the best, we strive to maintain sustainable partnerships. Our partnered farmers implement standardized farming practices, resulting in high-quality spices. Our products go through a stringent Quality Control process to ensure quality and safety until they reach their destination city or country. Every purchase of at least 30 tons will receive a special discount.

Our Products

Ginger

Ginger is widely used in traditional medicine and cuisine. It can be provided as dried slices or powder.

Turmeric

Turmeric is an essential spice, available in slices, polished form, or powder, widely known for its health benefits.

Black Pepper

Known for its bold flavor, Black Pepper is available in whole or ground form, perfect for seasoning.

White Pepper

White Pepper offers a milder taste, often used in light-colored dishes like sauces or soups.

Cinnamon

Cinnamon is highly fragrant and is used in both sweet and savory dishes, available as sticks or powder.

Nutmeg

Nutmeg adds a warm, spicy flavor to recipes, available as whole nutmegs or ground powder.

Cardamom

Known as the "Queen of Spices," cardamom is a versatile spice available as pods or powder.

Clove

Clove is used in both culinary and medicinal applications, offering a strong and pungent flavor.

Coffe

Coffee is a popular beverage known for its rich, aromatic flavor and stimulating effects, used in various forms like espresso and latte.

The Root of Pasak Bumi

The root of pasak bumi is famous for its aphrodisiac properties, considered an herbal medicine for male virility.

Galangal

Galangal is a spice with a spicy taste and distinctive aroma, commonly used in Southeast Asian cuisine and traditional medicine.

Mitragyna Speciosa

Mitragyna Speciosa, known by its scientific name Mitragyna speciosa, has long been used by people in several Southeast Asian regions including Indonesia for traditional medical purposes. The leaves are believed to have analgesic, stimulant, and opioid addictive effects.
